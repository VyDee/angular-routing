Preloading Feature Modules
    ** Lazy Loading
        > Launch app
            > Download imported modules     
                > template appears
                    > navigate to a lazy loaded Feature
                        > download lazy loaded module
                            > template appears
    
    ** Preloading (Eager Lazy Loading)
        > Launch app
            > Download imported modules     
                > template appears
                    > preload modules
                        > navigate to lazy loaded Feature
                            > template appears
